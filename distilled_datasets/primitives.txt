# NSM PRIMITIVES AND THEIR EMOTIONAL MAPPINGS
# ==============================================================================

# --- SUBSTANTIVES ---
I (self): Joy, Pride, Shame, Anxiety, Fear
you: Admiration, Adoration, Romance, Sympathy, Anger
someone: Admiration, Sympathy, Envy, Adoration
people: Admiration, Sympathy, Contempt (if "bad people"), Awe
something/thing: Interest, Curiosity, Craving
body: Calmness, Excitement, Fear (if threatened), Satisfaction

# --- RELATIONAL SUBSTANTIVES ---
kind: Admiration, Adoration, Love, Sympathy
part: Interest, Confusion (if partial understanding)

# --- DETERMINERS ---
this: Interest, Awe, Anxiety (if threatening)
the same: Calmness, Satisfaction, Nostalgia, Admiration
other/else: Curiosity, Anxiety, Awe, Envy, Interest

# --- QUANTIFIERS ---
one: Interest, Confusion (singular vs plural)
two: Interest, Romance (pair bond)
some: Curiosity, Hope, Craving
all: Awe, Admiration, Overwhelm, Sympathy
much/many: Awe, Excitement, Anxiety, Overwhelm
little/few: Sadness, Boredom, Calmness, Nostalgia

# --- EVALUATORS ---
good: Joy, Satisfaction, Admiration, Calmness
bad: Disgust, Sadness, Fear, Horror, Anger

# --- DESCRIPTORS ---
big: Awe, Fear, Admiration, Overwhelm
small: Adoration, Calmness, Interest, Nostalgia

# --- MENTAL PREDICATES ---
think: Interest, Confusion, Curiosity, Awe
know: Satisfaction, Calmness, Pride, Anxiety (if dangerous knowledge)
want: Craving, Desire, Excitement, Anxiety
don't want: Disgust, Fear, Anxiety, Calmness
feel: (core emotion primitive) - maps to ALL emotions
see: Aesthetic Appreciation, Interest, Awe, Fear, Joy
hear: Aesthetic Appreciation, Interest, Awe, Fear, Calmness

# --- SPEECH ---
say: Interest, Confusion, Anxiety, Triumph (if successful)
words: Interest, Confusion, Aesthetic Appreciation
true: Satisfaction, Calmness, Admiration

# --- ACTIONS, EVENTS, MOVEMENT ---
do: Excitement, Triumph, Pride, Anxiety
happen: Awe, Fear, Surprise, Interest
move: Excitement, Awe, Fear, Interest

# --- EXISTENCE, POSSESSION ---
be (somewhere): Calmness, Anxiety, Awe
there is: Curiosity, Awe, Interest, Excitement
be (someone/something): Identity → Joy, Pride, Sadness, Anxiety
(is) mine: Satisfaction, Pride, Envy, Anxiety

# --- LIFE AND DEATH ---
live: Joy, Excitement, Awe, Gratitude, Fear
die: Horror, Fear, Sadness, Awe, Nostalgia

# --- TIME ---
when/time: Nostalgia, Anxiety, Awe, Interest
now: Anxiety, Excitement, Calmness, Interest
before: Nostalgia, Regret, Pride, Sadness
after: Relief, Satisfaction, Nostalgia, Sadness
a long time: Nostalgia, Awe, Boredom, Calmness
a short time: Excitement, Anxiety, Interest, Urgency
for some time: Interest, Nostalgia, Patience, Anxiety
moment: Awe, Excitement, Fear, Interest

# --- SPACE ---
where/place: Curiosity, Nostalgia, Awe, Anxiety
here: Calmness, Nostalgia, Safety, Anxiety
above: Awe, Admiration, Fear (if extreme)
below: Awe, Curiosity, Fear, Interest
far: Nostalgia, Longing, Awe, Anxiety
near: Calmness, Safety, Anxiety, Intimacy
side: Interest, Confusion
inside: Comfort, Safety, Curiosity, Claustrophobia (fear)
touch/contact: Intimacy, Romance, Disgust (if unwanted), Joy

# --- LOGICAL CONCEPTS ---
not: (negation - context-dependent) → can invert any emotion
maybe: Hope, Anxiety, Curiosity, Interest
can: Excitement, Interest, Calmness, Anxiety
because: Interest, Understanding, Confusion
if: Hope, Anxiety, Curiosity, Interest

# --- INTENSIFIER, AUGMENTOR ---
very: Amplifies current emotion (Awe if extreme)
more: Interest, Craving, Excitement, Envy

# --- SIMILARITY ---
like/as/way: Aesthetic Appreciation, Admiration, Calmness, Nostalgia
